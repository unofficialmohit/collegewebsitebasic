Management Department
The Management programme in BITS is consistently ranked among the very best in the country by various survey reports. The latest survey done by Outlook India for 2018 ranks the MBA program offered by the Department of Management, BITS Pilani in the top 20 in India. BITS Pilani ranks among the top 10 private institutes offering management program in India. It is one of the top 10 institutes in North India offering management education. According to the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) report 2018, under the aegis of Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India, the management program offered by the institute is within the top 50 in the country.
